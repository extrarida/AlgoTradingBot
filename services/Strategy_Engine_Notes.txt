FILE: services/strategy_engine.py
LOCATION: services/ folder

WHAT IT DOES:
This is the brain of the bot. It runs all 40 trading strategies at the
same time on the latest price data, counts how many voted BUY versus
SELL, and decides whether to generate a trading signal.

HOW IT HELPS:
No single strategy alone triggers a trade. The engine requires multiple
strategies to agree before acting. This significantly reduces false
signals and bad trades.

HOW THE VOTING WORKS:
1. All 40 strategies run on the current price data.
2. Each strategy returns BUY, SELL, or NONE.
3. The engine counts the votes on each side.
4. If BUY votes >= 3 AND average confidence >= 60%, it signals BUY.
5. If SELL votes >= 3 AND average confidence >= 60%, it signals SELL.
6. Otherwise it signals NONE and no trade is placed.

KEY OUTPUTS:
  - final_signal: BUY, SELL, or NONE
  - confidence: Average confidence score of the winning strategies
  - buy_votes: How many strategies voted BUY
  - sell_votes: How many strategies voted SELL
  - none_votes: How many strategies voted NONE
  - top_strategies: The top 5 most confident strategies that voted

EXAMPLE:
  25 strategies vote BUY with 72% average confidence.
  15 strategies vote SELL.
  Result: BUY signal generated (25 votes > minimum 3, 72% > 60%).

SETTINGS YOU CAN CHANGE (in .env file):
  MIN_STRATEGY_VOTES=3      (how many must agree)
  CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD=0.60 (minimum confidence required)
