FILE: execution/risk_manager.py
LOCATION: execution/ folder

WHAT IT DOES:
This file is the safety guard for all trades. Before any trade is
placed, it runs through a series of checks. If any check fails,
the trade is blocked completely.

HOW IT HELPS:
It protects your account from large losses by enforcing strict rules
automatically. Even if the market signals are strong, the risk manager
can override and stop a trade from happening.

THE 5 CHECKS (run in order, first failure stops the trade):

  1. Kill Switch Check:
     Has the emergency stop button been pressed? If yes, no trades allowed.
     You can activate this from the dashboard to stop all trading instantly.

  2. Daily Limit Check:
     Has the bot placed the maximum allowed trades today? Default is 20.
     If yes, trading stops until the next day.

  3. Drawdown Check:
     Has your account dropped more than 5% from its highest point?
     If yes, trading stops to protect remaining capital.

  4. Lot Size Check:
     Is the requested lot size valid? Must be greater than 0 and not
     exceed the maximum lot size set in settings.

  5. Fat Finger Check:
     Is the order value more than 50% of your total equity? If yes,
     it blocks the trade to prevent accidental large orders.

POSITION SIZING:
The risk manager also calculates the correct lot size based on your
account balance and stop loss distance, ensuring you only risk a set
percentage (default 1%) of your account per trade.

KEY FUNCTIONS:
  - validate_trade(): Runs all 5 checks. Returns approved or rejected.
  - calc_lot_size(): Calculates safe lot size based on account equity.
  - activate_kill_switch(): Immediately stops all trading.
  - record_trade(): Counts each trade toward the daily limit.
  - current_drawdown_pct(): Shows current drawdown percentage.

SETTINGS YOU CAN CHANGE (in .env file):
  MAX_TRADES_PER_DAY=20
  MAX_DRAWDOWN_PCT=5.0
  RISK_PER_TRADE_PCT=1.0
  DEFAULT_LOT_SIZE=0.01
  MAX_LOT_SIZE=1.0
