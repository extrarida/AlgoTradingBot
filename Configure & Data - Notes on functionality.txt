The entire AlgoBot system has 14 layers. The config/ and data/ folders own the first 6 layers — everything that happens before a single strategy is evaluated:

Functions:
- Loads all settings — risk limits, DB URL, JWT secrets, strategy thresholds
- Connects to MT5 broker, receives raw price stream
- Market Data Connector
- Decodes raw broker feed into structured OHLCV data
- Maps broker field names to internal standard schema
- Validates data quality — no duplicates, no stale feeds
- Serves clean OHLCV DataFrames to strategies on demand

These form part of the first 6 layers of the total 14 layer architecture
